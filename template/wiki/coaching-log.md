# Coaching log

Tracks the lessons the agent has coached and whether they've been accepted, so coaching graduates
from explained to silent. After a lesson is accepted ~3×, the agent stops narrating it and just
applies the fix (anti-nag). The weekly review reports coaching *patterns*, not instances.

One row per lesson:

| Lesson | Standard | Accepted count | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| _e.g. risks arriving without an owner_ | `standards/risk-quality.md` | 0 | teaching |

Statuses: `teaching` (still explained each time) → `graduated` (accepted ~3×, now applied silently).
