# Wiki index

The catalog of every narrative page in this Project Agent instance. The agent maintains it: whenever
a wiki page is created or renamed, it's listed here under the right heading. Register items are not
listed here — they live in `registers/` and surface through `views/`; the wiki links to them by ID.

## Foundation

_Charter, scope, governance model, team — the enduring shape of the project (`wiki/foundation/`)._

## Meetings

_Synthesised meeting notes, one page per meeting (`wiki/meetings/`); the verbatim record stays in
`raw/sources/meetings/`._

## Governance

_Steering / board narrative: what each forum decided and what's outstanding (`wiki/governance/`)._

## People

_Optional narrative pages for key stakeholders (`wiki/people/`); the assessment of record is the
`STK-###` register item._

## Workstreams

_Per-workstream narrative — progress, issues, dependencies (`wiki/workstreams/`)._

## Answers

_Filed answers worth keeping: analyses, comparisons, weekly reviews (`wiki/answers/`)._
